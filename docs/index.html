The Problem

Playing modded games with friends means shared zip files, outdated mods, and broken sessions.

  • Missing mods crash multiplayer
  • Wrong versions cause incompatibility
  • Manual copying takes hours
  • Cloud uploads are painfully slow

The Solution

SyncCrate scans both sides, shows differences, and transfers only what changed.

  • Everyone gets the same mods and versions
  • 100-900 MB/s transfer speed over LAN
  • No servers, no accounts, no tracking
  • Works with Tailscale and ZeroTier too

Features

Built for Modders

Everything you need to keep your game mods in sync with friends.

🌐

Peer-to-Peer

Files transfer directly between computers at 100-900 MB/s. Nothing leaves your network. No cloud, no uploads.

⚡

Smart Diffing

SHA-256 hash comparison. Only transfers what's different — identical files are skipped automatically.

🛡️

Conflict Resolution

Keep yours, use theirs, or keep both. Every conflict is resolved on your terms before any files move.

🏷️

Tags & Search

Organize mods with built-in tags. Bulk-tag, filter, and search across your entire collection.

📦

Profiles & Sharing

Snapshot your mod setup, export as a .synccrate-profile file, share on Discord. Friends drag it in and they're set.

💾

Backup & Restore

Full snapshot of your game content. Safety backup created automatically before every restore.

🔒

Privacy First

No servers, no accounts, no tracking, no data collection. Files stay between you and your friends.

🔍

Auto-Detection

Automatically finds your game folders. Detects installed DLC, packs, and checks mod compatibility.

⚠️

Script Warnings

Flags potentially risky files (.ts4script, .lua, .jar, .dll) per game before syncing so you can review them.

How It Works

Four Steps. Zero Config.

No setup files, no port forwarding, no accounts. Works on any network where devices can see each other.

1

Host

Start a session and broadcast on your network.

2

Join

Friends discover your session and connect instantly.

3

Compare

SyncCrate scans both sides and shows what's different.

4

Sync

Resolve conflicts, hit sync. Files transfer at LAN speed.
