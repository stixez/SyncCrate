Why SimShare?

Everything you need to keep your Sims 4 mods in sync with friends.

🌐

Peer-to-Peer

Files transfer directly between computers at full LAN speed. Nothing leaves your network.

🔍

Auto-Discovery

Finds other SimShare users on your network automatically via mDNS. No IPs to configure.

⚡

Smart Diffing

Compares SHA-256 hashes. Only transfers what's actually different — identical files are skipped.

👥

Multi-Peer

One host, multiple friends. Everyone syncs independently at the same time.

📁

Mod Profiles

Snapshot your mod setup. Export and share profiles as .simshare-profile files.

🛡

Conflict Resolution

Keep yours, use theirs, or keep both. Every conflict is resolved on your terms.

See It in Action

How It Works

Four steps. No setup, no accounts, no configuration files.

1

Host

One person starts a session and begins broadcasting on the network.

2

Join

Friends scan for hosts and connect with one click.

3

Compare

SimShare scans both mod folders and shows what's different.

4

Sync

Resolve conflicts, hit sync, and files transfer at LAN speed.
